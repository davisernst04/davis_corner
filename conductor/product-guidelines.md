# Product Guidelines: Personal Blog App\n\n## Visual Language\n- **Minimalist Aesthetic:** Emphasize clean lines, ample white space, and clear, high-quality typography for a professional look.\n- **Responsive Design:** Mobile-first approach to ensure a consistent experience across all devices.\n- **Accessibility:** Adherence to WCAG standards for readability and easy navigation.\n\n## Tone and Voice\n- **Conversational & Approachable:** Content should be written in a direct, friendly manner that balances expertise with clarity.\n- **Friendly & Supportive:** The blog's personality should feel warm and accessible to readers.\n\n## User Experience (UX)\n- **Speed & Performance:** Prioritize fast page loads and smooth navigation using Next.js.\n- **Focused Simplicity:** Eliminate distractions to provide a focused and pleasant reading experience.\n- **Interactive Feedback:** Use rich hover states, loading skeletons, and clear alerts to provide meaningful feedback during interactions.
